What is Pragmatica?

VR Learning Platform

Professionally curated VR activities that allow you to practice your communication skills in a safe, meaningful, 3D environment. The activities allow you to extend practice beyond your therapy session and increase the intensity of your practice. You can learn at your own pace an get tips and feedback to help you along the way.

Data Dashboard

Speech language pathologists, behavior analysts and other communication professionals can moniter clients' progress and performance with data collected when clients go through activities. Therapists can assign activities and adjust parameters to create a fully customized experience for their clients and follow their progress over time.

Pragmatica is commited to helping more people get access to the speech therapy they deserve. It enables clients and learners to access real-life functional scenarios through VR as a way to practice their goals and increase the intensity of their language therapy.
