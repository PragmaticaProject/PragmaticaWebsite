Stuck in waitlists? Can't find a therapist?

Pragmatica can get you the help you need. Our VR solution allows you to get immediate intervention so that you can practice your communication skills without the need of a therapist next to you.

Compared to traditional therapy, Pragmatica offers:

  • Professionally curated and tailor made activities to help you meet your goals
  • Practice your communication skills in a safe, meaningful, 3D environment
  • Increase the intensity of your practice beyond your therapy session
  • Learn at your own pace with tips and feedback to help you along the way
