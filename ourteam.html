Why we're the right people for the job

We have a team of passionate individuals with the mission of making speech therapy more accessible.

Karthik and Thomas are recent graduates of the Management Engineering program at the University of Waterloo. They have worked together for years and have the technical experience in S/W development and user experience to build a great product in a Virtual Reality (VR) medium.

Shawna and Brittany are professional speech and language pathologists (SLP's) who have years of experience in the field and run a licensed practice together. They understand what all needs to go into the product to truly make it helpful to its users. They curate all the activitiesd in the product and ensure that it is both helpful and safe to use.
