What is Pragmatica?

VR Learning Platform

Professionally curated VR activities that allow you to practice your communication skills in a safe, meaningful, 3D environment. The activities allow you to extend practice beyond your therapy session and increase the intensity of your practice. You can learn at your own pace an get tips and feedback to help you along the way.

Data Dashboard

Speech language pathologists, behavior analysts and other communication professionals can moniter clients' progress and performance with data collected when clients go through activities. Therapists can assign activities and adjust parameters to create a fully customized experience for their clients and follow their progress over time.

For Clinicians:

- Helps clinicians manage their caseload better by automating parts of therapy sessions

- Track your clients' progress and make data-driven decisions about next steps in treatment

- Offers value-based healthcare using key metrics of patients' performance during activities

- Provides standardized treatment plans for clinicians to use with clients

For Patients:

- fun, engaging, and interactive tool that allows users to target language therapy goals in a functional, meaningful way

- Product designed for both children and adults with communication challenges

- Allows users to practice their communication skills from the comfort of their own home

- Users and their parents/partners can track their progress and see their skills grow over time.
