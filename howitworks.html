What is Pragmatica?

VR Learning Platform

Professionally curated VR activities where you can practice your communication skills in a safe 3d-environment without a therapist present. Activities are tailor- made to help achieve communication goals and contain tips and feedback to create a smooth experience.

Data Dashboard

Speech therapists can moniter clients' progress and their performance with key metrics tracked when clients go through activities. Therapists can assign activities and adjust parameters to create a fully customized experience for their clients and follow their progress over time.

For Therapists:

- Reduce waitlists and handle a lot more patients by seeing each one less frequently

- Offer value-based healthcare using key metrics of patients' performance during activities

For Parents:

- Have your child practice their communication skills from the comfort of their own home

- Understand your child's performance and their progress over time through the data dashboard
