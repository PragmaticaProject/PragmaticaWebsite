How It Works

For Clinicians:

  • Helps clinicians manage their caseload better by automating parts of therapy sessions
  • Track your clients' progress and make data-driven decisions about next steps in treatment
  • Offers value-based healthcare using key metrics of patients' performance during activities
  • Provides standardized treatment plans for clinicians to use with clients

For Patients:

  • fun, engaging, and interactive tool that allows users to target language therapy goals in a functional, meaningful way
  • Product designed for both children and adults with communication challenges
  • Allows users to practice their communication skills from the comfort of their own home
  • Users and their parents/partners can track their progress and see their skills grow over time.
